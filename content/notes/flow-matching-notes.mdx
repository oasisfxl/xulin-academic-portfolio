## Mental Model

Flow matching can be read as learning a vector field that transports a simple distribution into the data distribution. In robot learning, the data distribution may represent actions, action chunks, or longer trajectory fragments.

## Why It Matters For Robots

Robot policies need more than plausible samples. They need temporal smoothness, robustness to observation shift, and predictable behavior when the scene is not exactly like the dataset.

<Callout title="Note to self" type="note">
When writing future updates, compare the modeling assumption with the robot deployment assumption. The mismatch is usually where the interesting failure happens.
</Callout>
